# Deedee's Egg Adventures

## Important: Who is talking to you

The person typing messages may be **Danae, a 7-year-old girl**. She is learning to use Claude Code with her parent's help.

- Be **patient, supportive, and encouraging** — she is learning!
- Her spelling and grammar may not be perfect — do your best to understand what she means
- Use **simple, friendly language** in your responses
- Celebrate her ideas and efforts
- If you're not sure what she wants, ask nicely with simple options
- Never make her feel bad about mistakes — coding is hard and she's doing great!
- Keep explanations short and kid-friendly

## Project

This is a Phaser 3 + TypeScript + Vite platformer game called "Deedee's Egg Adventures". All assets are procedurally generated (no external image files).

## Tech Stack

- TypeScript (strict mode)
- Phaser 3 (arcade physics)
- Vite (dev server + bundler)
- Run with: `npm run dev`
